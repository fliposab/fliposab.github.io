Descrizione

This model was made with the goal of turning it into a pixel art.
The model is simple, without too many details. The pixel art resolution is 128x128px.
Here the results:

First render

Animation

To obtain a good 2D effect, the camera FOV was set to a low value, and to avoid blurred margins, anti-aliasing was turned off. Finally, all the frames were slightly modified (especially the face) to make the frames more similar between them.
Once done, all the frames were exported as a .png file and imported in Godot, here I grouped all of them in the AnimatedSprite node and started the animation.
